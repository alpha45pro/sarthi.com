How Sarthi Works

A simple yet powerful system designed to respond within seconds when you need it most.

1

Easy Installation

Simply attach the Sarthi device to your car's dashboard or windshield using the provided mount. Pair it with the mobile app.

2

Continuous Monitoring

Sarthi continuously monitors your vehicle using multiple sensors to detect sudden impacts, rollovers, or other accident indicators.

3

Accident Detection

When an accident is detected, Sarthi initiates a brief countdown, allowing you to cancel the alert if it's a false alarm.

4

Emergency Response

If the alert isn't canceled, Sarthi automatically contacts emergency services and sends notifications to your emergency contacts.
